I'm Xiao Xing — a creative technologist and visual artist working across code, physical materials, and storytelling. My practice is rooted in deep, abstract thinking and the belief that a project should be felt before it is understood. I work in whatever form the idea demands — immersive installation, interactive web experience, sculpture, photography, film — and I find as much meaning in the process of making as in the finished work.

Collaboration is central to how I think. I'm drawn to projects that challenge assumptions, invite hands-on experimentation, and leave room for the unexpected. Whether I'm building with Arduino, writing in JavaScript, or layering plaster by hand, the question is always the same: can this carry genuine feeling? Visual storytelling is what drives everything — the conviction that a project, in any medium, can tell its story through atmosphere alone.
